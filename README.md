Visualizing the Care Gap
===

The following repo contains experiments visualizing the care gap in Northern Vermont, specifically with access to pharmacies.

## Background
 
For many years Hardwick had a pharmacy, though its name changed frequently.
In its final incarnation, as a Walgreens, quality of service declined and the store eventually closed, along with many other Walgreens locations across the country.

This left a gap in critical care needs in Hardwick and the surrounding community,
more than doubling drive times to a pharmacy for most people.

In 2026, NVRH received over $1 million in funding to address the care gap in Caledonia County,
and hopefully bring a pharmacy back to Hardwick.

## Overview

This repo contains experiments in visualizing the care gap across Northern Vermont (Lamoille, Orleans, Washington, and Caledonia counties).
This shows the critical need in Hardwick, but also highlights that other rural communities in the area are still underserved,
in particular to the northwest of Hardwick, around Lowell, and to the southeast, in Peacham/Barnet.
